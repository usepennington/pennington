Adding a second post gives the archive and tag pages something to sort. Posts
with shared tags cluster together on `/tags/<name>` pages.

Set `isDraft: true` in front matter to hide a post from listings while you work
on it — it still renders at its URL, it just doesn't show up in archive or
feed.
