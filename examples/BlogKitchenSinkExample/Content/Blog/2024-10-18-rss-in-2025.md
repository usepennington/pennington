The audience for a personal blog's RSS feed is small but loyal. The cost is
a single config flag — `EnableRss = true` — and Pennington wires the channel
metadata, item ordering, and the `application/rss+xml` content type with
no further input.

## What you get without trying

Posts ordered by `date` descending, drafts filtered out, the canonical
URL composed from `CanonicalBaseUrl`, and an `<atom:link rel="self">` for
feed-reader autodiscovery. The site header link comes from `MainSiteLinks`
once you add an entry pointing at `/rss.xml`.
