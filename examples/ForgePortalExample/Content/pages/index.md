Welcome to **Forge**, the internal developer portal for our organization. Forge brings our documentation, blog, and release notes together in one place.

## Start here

- [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/) — set up your local environment and run your first service.
- [API Keys](/docs/api-keys/) — request credentials for staging and production.
- [Pipeline Configuration](/docs/pipeline-config/) — wire up builds and deploys.

## What's new

Head over to the [blog](/blog/welcome/) for product announcements, retrospectives, and engineering notes from the platform team.

Release notes for every shipped version live under `/releases/` — the newest build is always at the top.

## Need help?

Ping us in `#forge` on Slack, or [read about the team](/about/).
