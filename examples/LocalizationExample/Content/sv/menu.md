title: Zee Menoo
order: 3
---

Ifery guud tefern needs a menoo. Bork bork bork! Here-a's vhet ve-a're-a serfeeng tudey.

## Eppeteezers

| Deesh | Descreepshun | Preece-a |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Breed & Bootter | Fressh beked, verm frum zee oofee | 3 guld |
| Cheese-a Plete-a | A selecshun ooff eged cheeses | 5 guld |
| Suoop ooff zee Dey | Esk yuoor bermeid | 4 guld |

## Meeen Cuurses

### Zee Edffuntooorer's Pletter
A herty purshun ooff ruested meet, putetues, und seesunl fegetebbles. Bork bork bork! Cumes veet a tenkerd ooff ele-a.

### Zee Veezerd's Deleeght
A mysteeriuoos stoo thet chunges flefur veet ifery beete-a. Seede-a ifffects mey incloode-a tempurery lefeeteshun. Bork!

### Zee Berd's Bool
A culurfful seled tupped veet ideeeble-a floooers und a huney feenegreeette-a. Inspeeres pueetry in 3 oooot ooff 4 deeners.

## Desserts

- **Dregun's Breet Ceke-a** -- Speeecy chuclete-a ceke-a veet cheeli frusteeeng. Bork!
- **Ilfee Pooddeeng** -- Leeght, iteereeeel, und sleeeghtly sperkly
- **Dverffee Stuoot Fluet** -- Derk stuoot veet fenilla ice-a creem

## Befereeges

> Ell befereeges ere-a serfed in epprupreeeetely seezed fessels fur yuoor speceees. Bork bork bork!
