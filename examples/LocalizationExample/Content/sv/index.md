## Vhet Ve-a Serfe-a

Ve-a hefe-a a menoo ooff knooledge-a, lufeengly transleted intu:

- **Ingleesh** -- zee huoose-a cummun tunge-a
- **Peeg Leteen** -- fur zee clesseecelly treeened
- **Svedeesh Cheff** -- fur thuse-a vhu prefer zeeur docs veet a seede-a ooff bork bork bork!
- **Pirete-a** -- fur zee seeferreeng fulk

## Hoo It Vurks

Thees seete-a uses **fulder-besed lucele-a ruoteeng**. Zee deffeoolt lungooege-a (Ingleesh) leefes et zee ruut, vheele-a trunsleshuns leefe-a in soobfulders.

Try sveetcheeng lungooeges useeng zee lungooege-a peecker in zee tup-reeght curner! Bork bork bork!
