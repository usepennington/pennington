## Main Courses

### The Adventurer's Platter
A hearty portion of roasted meat, potatoes, and seasonal vegetables. Comes with a tankard of ale.

### The Wizard's Delight
A mysterious stew that changes flavor with every bite. Side effects may include temporary levitation.

### The Bard's Bowl
A colorful salad topped with edible flowers and a honey vinaigrette. Inspires poetry in 3 out of 4 diners.

## Desserts

- **Dragon's Breath Cake** -- Spicy chocolate cake with chili frosting
- **Elven Pudding** -- Light, ethereal, and slightly sparkly
- **Dwarven Stout Float** -- Dark stout with vanilla ice cream

## Beverages
