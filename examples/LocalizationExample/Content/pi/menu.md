## Main Courses

### The Adventurer's Platter
A hearty portion o' roasted beast, potatoes, an' whatever vegetables we plundered. Comes with a tankard o' grog!

### The Wizard's Delight
A mysterious stew that changes flavor with every bite. Side effects may include temporary flyin' -- useful fer boardin' enemy vessels!

### The Bard's Bowl
A colorful salad topped with edible flowers an' honey vinaigrette. Inspires sea shanties in 3 out o' 4 diners, arrr!

## Desserts

- **Dragon's Breath Cake** -- Spicy chocolate cake with chili frostin'. Hot as cannon fire!
- **Elven Pudding** -- Light, ethereal, an' slightly sparkly. Like mermaid tears.
- **Dwarven Stout Float** -- Dark stout with vanilla ice cream. A proper pirate's treat!

## Beverages
