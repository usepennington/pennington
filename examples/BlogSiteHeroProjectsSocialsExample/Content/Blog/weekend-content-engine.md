The homepage surfaces taught in this tutorial all sit above the recent-posts
list, so the blog needs at least one post for the page to look like a real
homepage. This is that post.

## What the homepage shows

With `HeroContent` populated, the top of `/` leads with the headline and
supporting paragraph instead of a bare post list. `MyWork` renders the
right-rail "My Work" card. `Socials` renders the icon row underneath it.
`MainSiteLinks` fills the top-nav in the page header.
