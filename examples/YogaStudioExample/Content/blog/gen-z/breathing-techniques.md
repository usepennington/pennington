Okay so hear me out — breathing is literally the most underrated wellness hack. Like, we all do it already but most of us are doing it wrong. No cap.

Here are five techniques I teach at 937 Yoga that are absolutely bussin' for stress.

## 1. Box Breathing (the Navy SEAL one)

This is giving main character energy. Navy SEALs literally use this before missions. You can use it before that presentation that's giving you the ick.

> [!TIP]
> Start with 4-count cycles. Once that hits different, level up to 6 or 8.

**The vibe:**
- Breathe in for 4 counts
- Hold it for 4 counts
- Let it out for 4 counts
- Hold empty for 4 counts

Do this 4-8 times and tell me your anxiety doesn't leave the chat.

## 2. Extended Exhale (the chill pill)

When your exhale is longer than your inhale, your nervous system literally goes into rest mode. It's science, bestie.

**The vibe:**
- Breathe in for 4
- Breathe out for 6-8

This is THE move for anyone whose brain won't stop running.

## 3. Alternate Nostril Breathing (sounds weird, works tho)

I know this sounds unhinged but stay with me. It balances both sides of your brain. The research is valid — it reduces blood pressure and boosts focus.

> [!NOTE]
> If your nose is congested, no stress. Just imagine the air flowing through both sides. Manifestation energy.

## 4. Lion's Breath (the one that looks chaotic)

This one has zero chill and that's the whole point. You literally stick your tongue out and go "HAAA." It releases all the tension in your jaw and face where you've been clenching all day.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Maybe don't do this at your desk unless you want to become a meme. Save it for the mat fr fr.

## 5. 4-7-8 Breathing (the sleep hack)

If you can't sleep this technique is literally a cheat code. Two rounds and you're basically unconscious (in a good way).

**The vibe:**
- In through nose for 4
- Hold for 7
- Out through mouth for 8
