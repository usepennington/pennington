Okay so you're thinking about trying yoga but the vibes are giving anxious. Valid. I was the exact same way before my first class.

Spoiler: it was fine. Actually it was lowkey amazing.

## What Yoga Actually Is (the real tea)

Yoga is NOT about being bendy. It's about connecting your breath to your body. The flexibility, the strength, the wild poses — that's all bonus content.

## Picking Your First Class

Not all yoga is the same. Here's the breakdown, no gatekeeping:

> [!TIP]
> Our Saturday **Community Flow** at 937 Yoga is literally designed for first-timers. The vibes are immaculate.

### Beginner-Friendly (go for these)

**Vinyasa Flow (All Levels)** — The bread and butter. Flowing movements, good music, modifications for days. It's giving accessible.

**Yin Yoga** — Slow stretching, zero athletic ability needed. You hold poses for ages and let gravity do everything. Peak lazy girl energy (complimentary).

**Restorative** — It's like a nap but therapeutic. You lie on props and exist. If you think you "can't do yoga," this will humble that narrative.

### Save for Later

**Ashtanga** — The grind. A set sequence that levels up over months. Fire once you know basics.

**Hot Yoga** — 95 degrees plus new poses = a lot. Build your foundation first.

**Power Yoga** — It's a workout cosplaying as yoga. Sick once you know what you're doing.

## What Happens in Your First Class

### Before
- Show up 10-15 min early
- Wear something you can move in (no jeans, period)
- Tell the instructor it's your first time (they'll look out for you)

### During
- You WILL forget which is left and which is right. It's universal.
- You WILL peek at your neighbor. That's literally how humans learn.
- You might get emotional out of nowhere. More normal than you'd think.
- If something hurts, stop. Challenge ≠ pain.

### After
- You'll feel like a new person
- You'll be weirdly sore tomorrow
- You'll wonder why you didn't start sooner

## Myths That Need to Be Unalived

> [!WARNING]
> These myths are literally keeping people from the mat. Let's debunk.

**"I'm not flexible enough"** — That's like saying you're too hungry to eat. Yoga CREATES flexibility, bestie.

**"It's just stretching"** — Tell that to someone leaving James's Power Yoga class looking absolutely deceased.

**"I need special equipment"** — Comfy clothes + showing up. We literally provide everything else.

**"Yoga is only for women"** — Yoga was invented by men and practiced exclusively by men for thousands of years. Everyone belongs.

## Your First Month Roadmap

**Week 1:** One class. Any "All Levels" class.

**Week 2:** Two classes. Try a different vibe.

**Week 3:** Notice what you're into. Yin? Vinyasa? Meditation?

**Week 4:** Lock in your routine. 2-3x/week is the sweet spot.
