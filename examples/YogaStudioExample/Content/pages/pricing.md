## Student & Senior Discount

Full-time students and seniors (65+) receive 20% off all memberships and class packs. Just bring valid ID on your first visit.

## Corporate Wellness

We partner with local businesses to offer group rates and on-site classes. If your company is interested in a wellness program, [reach out](/contact) and we'll put together a custom proposal.

## New Student Special

**30 days of unlimited yoga for $59.** Available to first-time students only. No strings attached — if you love it, switch to a monthly plan. If not, no hard feelings.

## Refund Policy

Monthly memberships can be paused for up to 30 days per year (life happens). Class packs never expire and are fully transferable. Drop-in payments are non-refundable but can be applied to a pack purchase within 7 days.
