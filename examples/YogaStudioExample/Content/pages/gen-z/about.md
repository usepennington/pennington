## The Lore

937 Yoga started in 2019 in a converted warehouse — house number 937 on Elm Street. What began as Maya Chen's tiny teaching space (like, 8 mats max) has evolved into Portland's most welcoming yoga community. No gatekeeping, just good energy.

We kept the name because it's a vibe check on our origins: small, personal, real.

## The Philosophy

We believe yoga belongs to everyone. Full stop. Not just the flexible girlies, the athletes, or the people with perfect handstands. Every single body that walks through our door deserves to feel supported, challenged, and seen.

Our teaching is rooted in tradition but not stuck in it. We honor the lineages while making space for modern bodies, modern stress, and modern science. It's giving respectful evolution.

## What Makes Us Hit Different

**No mirrors.** We removed them day one. Your practice should be felt, not watched. Main character energy comes from within.

**Real community.** We know your name. We celebrate your wins. We show up for each other. It's giving found family.

**Evidence-based teaching.** Our instructors stay current on anatomy, physiology, and research. Ancient wisdom meets peer-reviewed studies.

**Accessible pricing.** Because money shouldn't gatekeep wellness. Period. Check our [pricing page](/pricing).

## The Space

Three studios in a light-filled, renovated building:

- **Main Studio** — Biggest room, 30 mats. Bamboo floors, tall ceilings, natural light. The vibes are immaculate.
- **Hot Room** — Purpose-built for heated classes. Proper ventilation so it's challenging but not unhinged.
- **Zen Room** — The quiet zone. Yin, restorative, meditation. Dimmed lights, sound-dampened walls. Peak cozy.

Plus a lobby with tea, filtered water, changing rooms, and showers. We thought of everything.
