## Student & Senior Discount

Full-time students and seniors (65+) get 20% off everything. Just bring your ID the first time. We love supporting the whole community fr fr.

## Corporate Wellness

We collab with local businesses for group rates and on-site classes. If your company wants to invest in not burning out their employees, [hit us up](/contact).

## New Student Special

**30 days of unlimited yoga for $59.** First-timers only. No strings, no hidden fees, no ick. Love it? Switch to monthly. Not for you? Zero hard feelings.

## Refund Policy

Monthly plans can be paused for up to 30 days/year (because life be lifing). Class packs never expire and you can share them. Drop-in payments are non-refundable but can be applied toward a pack within 7 days. We keep it fair.
