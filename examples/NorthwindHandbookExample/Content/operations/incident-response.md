## Severity Levels

| Severity | Description | Response Time | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| SEV-1 | Complete outage | 15 minutes | All users cannot access the service |
| SEV-2 | Major degradation | 30 minutes | Key feature unavailable for many users |
| SEV-3 | Minor issue | 4 hours | Non-critical feature affected |
| SEV-4 | Cosmetic | Next business day | UI glitch with workaround |

## Response Steps

1. Acknowledge the alert
2. Assess severity and impact
3. Communicate status to stakeholders
4. Investigate and mitigate
5. Write post-incident review
