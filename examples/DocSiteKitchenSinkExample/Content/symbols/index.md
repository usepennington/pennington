These pages are served by a second markdown source registered against the
custom `ApiFrontMatter` record (see `ServiceConfiguration.RegisterApiSource`),
not the DocSite template's built-in `DocSiteFrontMatter`. That is what lets
them carry the extra `namespace` and `stability` keys.
