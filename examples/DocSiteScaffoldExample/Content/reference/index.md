# Reference

Welcome to the **Reference** area. Reference pages live under
`Content/reference/` and share nothing with Guides — the `reference` slug in
`DocSiteOptions.Areas` puts them on their own sidebar tab and under their own
top-level URL prefix.

Come here when you need authoritative, look-up-style material.
